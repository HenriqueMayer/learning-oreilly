### Isolation by LOB
- Creating separate workspaces for different **lines of business (LOB)**
    - **What is a Line of Business (LOB)?**
      - A distinct business unit within an organization, often with its own data and analytics needs.
    - **Benefits of Isolating by LOB**:
      - Enhanced security and data governance
      - Tailored resource allocation and cost management
      - Customized environment settings for specific business requirements
- Configuring workspace settings to meet specific LOB requirements
- Implementing data access policies tailored to each LOB
- Monitoring resource usage and costs per LOB
